Traveling to Portland

Where to stay

The Hoyt Arboretum, where the party will take place, is located in Washington Park in SW Portland. Fun, convenient neighborhoods to stay in are:

  • Downtown Portland
  • NW 23rd Ave shopping district
  • Slabtown
  • Pearl district

If you're trying to visit on the cheap, we have some space at our house or can set you up with a friend :)

Activities in Town

  • Explore Washington Park, including the Oregon Zoo
  • Hike in Forest Park or the Marquam Hill Nature Preserve
  • Check out art galleries in the Pearl or Alberta
  • Eat at food cart pods
  • Walk or bike the Willamette waterfront
  • Browse books at Powells
  • Experience OMSI, our science museum

Day Trips

  • Float the Sandy River at Oxbow Regional Park
  • Build sand castles on the Oregon Coast
  • Hike waterfalls and sample fruit in the Columbia River Gorge
  • Explore the lava tubes of Ape Caves on Mt. St. Helens
  • Visit the fish hatchery at Bonneville Dam
